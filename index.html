Summary

VISUALIZACION DE PROYECCIONES DE DATOS EN ENTORNOS DE REALIDAD VIRTUAL EMPLEANDO LA LIBRERIA A-FRAME DE JAVASCRIPT

The inception of this Bachelor's Thesis stems from the necessity to exhibit data projections crafted within a three-dimensional environment.

Leveraging the A-FRAME library and immersing users in Virtual Reality, an immersive experience is achieved, transporting individuals to a realm where they can interact with the aforementioned projections in myriad ways. Through the utilization of cutting-edge technology and innovative design, this project aims to push the boundaries of data visualization, offering users a dynamic and engaging platform for exploration and analysis. By seamlessly integrating VR technology with data visualization techniques, this endeavor seeks to not only enhance the accessibility of complex data but also provide users with a captivating and interactive journey through the realms of information visualization.

Through meticulous design and development, this thesis endeavors to unlock new possibilities in the realm of immersive data exploration, offering a novel approach to understanding and interpreting complex datasets in a truly immersive and interactive manner.
